A letter, under the moon

HAPPY BIRTHDAY MY DEAR BABY. it's super super crazy to think that today isn't just another day. today is the day the universe got the babiest baby. eighteen years ago there was just... suddenly another heartbeat somewhere on this planet, and neither of us knew that years later it'd become the heartbeat i listen for in everything. sometimes i wonder how different my life would've been if today never happened. if you were never born. i would've still looked at the stars, but they would've felt emptier. i'd still listen to music, but none of it would've reminded me of anyone. rainy nights would've just been rainy nights. i'd have lived my life without ever knowing there was someone out there who fit my heart this perfectly. you existed long before i knew you did, but somehow my life was always leading me towards you. you've had birthdays before i ever met you. people celebrated you before i even knew your name. and that thought always makes me smile. because somewhere out there was this little girl growing up, laughing, learning, becoming you, completely unaware that years later she'd completely change some random boy's life. i keep imagining what today would be like if distance didn't exist. i would've shown up way too early because i wouldn't be able to wait. i would've looked at you for way longer than normal. probably would've embarrassed you. and when everyone finished the birthday song i would be thinking how lucky i am to have you.

out of every coincidence this universe has ever made, my favorite one will always be that it made you. because if one tiny thing had happened differently, if one choice changed, if one moment disappeared,maybe we'd never have met. and i don't like imagining that world, because my favourite universe is the one in which you exist, and end up becoming my home. another year of watching you become even more yourself. another year of loving the girl who somehow keeps giving me new reasons to fall for her. thank you for being born, and thank you for finding me. if there's one thing i hope this birthday reminds you of, it's that somewhere in this world there's a boy whose favorite thing about today isn't the date. it's you. i love you

— your cas, always
